3.0 kWh Solar Generator

Posted on 2022-01-01 Edited on 2022-01-02

This project was completed at the beginning of 2021. It probably took 40 hours over a few months.

Most of this project is based off of LithiumSolar's video

I sourced recycled 2200 mAh modem cells from batteryhookup.com. It's tough to find used 2600 mAh cells, I do have some, but not the 364 required for this project. I ended up being a little less than 3.0 kWh after testing all the cells.

Ended up using kWeld to spot weld. Also sourced 4P nickel strip to simplify.
